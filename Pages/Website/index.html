Services that we can help you with

We can offer a comprehensive range of smart services that incorporate solar tracking panels, temperature and humidity sensors, heaters, and coolers, all of which can be conveniently controlled through a IoT based microcontroller which can be controlled through a phone application. Our solar tracking panels ensure that your solar panels are always pointed towards the sun, optimizing their energy output throughout the day.

Our temperature and humidity sensors provide you with real-time data on your storage environment, allowing you to adjust your heating and cooling systems accordingly for maximum energy efficiency. Our heaters and coolers can be remotely controlled via your phone, allowing you to set the perfect temperature for your home. With our smart services, you can have full control over your energy usage and optimize your overall environmental footprint.
